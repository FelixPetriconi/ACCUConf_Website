system https://www.conan.io/[Conan]. Package management in {cpp} is likely to be as big a "thing" in the
{cpp} community as {cpp}17, and so very important to the development of {cpp} software development. We are
therefore very please to announce that Conan is now a sponsor of ACCU 2017.
